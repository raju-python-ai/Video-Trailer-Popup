Movie Title : MERSAL

🎬 Mersal is a Tamil action thriller film starring Vijay in a triple role as a magician, a doctor, and a village hero. The story revolves around a quest for justice, where the magician seeks revenge against corrupt medical practitioners who caused his father's death and brother’s suffering. Through clever illusions and social activism, he exposes the dark side of the healthcare industry. The film flashes between past and present, revealing how a noble father and his sons were betrayed. Packed with action, emotion, and a strong social message, Mersal highlights the need for ethical medical practices and public accountability.

Movie Title : JANANAYAGAN

🎬 Jananayagan is an upcoming Tamil political action thriller film starring Thalapathy Vijay in what is expected to be his final acting role before entering full-time politics. Directed by H. Vinoth and produced by KVN Productions, the film presents Vijay as a powerful mass leader, possibly a police officer, who fights for justice and the voice of the people. With music composed by Anirudh Ravichander and a strong ensemble cast including Pooja Hegde, Bobby Deol, and Gautham Vasudev Menon, Jananayagan explores themes of democracy, leadership, and public empowerment. Scheduled for a grand Pongal release on January 9, 2026, the film is being released in multiple languages and is seen as both a cinematic experience and a symbolic farewell to Vijay’s film career as he transitions into his political journey.
